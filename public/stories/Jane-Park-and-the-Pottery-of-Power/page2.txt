The bright streets of the village shook with each step the villains took as they forced their way through, demanding tribute for their 'protection.' Bright blue skies turned dark under their ominous presence. Jane, however, stood strong in her little studio as she crafted clay into vessels of wonder, leaving her windows open to the whistling winds that told tales of bravery.

Her studio was a warm, welcoming place with walls adorned with prints of dragonflies and lilies. The smell of rich clay and aromatic herbs filled the air, and light streamed through the wide windows, bouncing off shelf after shelf of glowing, polished ceramic works.

Realizing her pottery was the only weapon she had, Jane bravely decided to stand against the corrupted heroes with her creations.
