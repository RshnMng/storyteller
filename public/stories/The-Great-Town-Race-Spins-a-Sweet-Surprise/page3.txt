They crossed the line, not in first, but second, with Yara leading them across triumphantly. Their unity caught the attention of Mr. Hopkins, the kind owner of the town's bike shop. Inspired by the group's spirit, he decided there was no better gift than rewarding all of them with an exciting surprise.

As the friends caught their breath, Mr. Hopkins approached. Clapping his hands, he announced, "You may not have won the race, but you've won something much more valuable—friendship and sportsmanship." As a reward, he surprised them with a bike designed for friendship—large enough for all five, built with their colors and symbols. Their eyes widened in excitement, and Mr. Hopkins added, "Plus, an ice cream party at Willow Creek's Sweet Spot!"
